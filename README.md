## 📖 Topics Covered

Based on the STAT 266 curriculum, notes in this repository include:

- Functions of random variables
- Sampling distributions
- Central Limit Theorem & Law of Large Numbers
- Statistical models for data
- Likelihood, parameters & their interpretation
- Objectives of statistical inference
- Point & interval estimation
- Method of moments
- Hypothesis testing — errors of the first and second kind
- Significance level, power, and p-values
